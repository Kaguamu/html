Our Story

Founded in 2020, The Little Taco Shop has quickly become a favorite spot for taco lovers. Our team is passionate about creating authentic Kenyan flavors and providing a warm, welcoming atmosphere for our customers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide delicious, high-quality tacos that bring joy to our customers. We believe in using only the freshest ingredients and traditional recipes to create a memorable dining experience.
